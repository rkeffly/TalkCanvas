    The idea behind the Forest Of Stars

    Step into a whole new world. This striking piece of wall art is designed by a best-selling artist and handcrafted with the highest quality materials. Forest Of Stars transports you around the world with breathtaking color and clarity spread across multiple canvases. The artist’s style draws you in with a unique perspective. Forest Of Stars is part of our Landscape Collection, and it will create a stunning focal point on your walls.

    Quality, Lasting Production

    All our products are carefully handcrafted and inspected for quality. Your canvas will arrive ready to hang with metal sawtooth hangers and rubber bumpers for a smooth fit on your wall.

    Art Gallery Level of Quality

    We produce perfect genuine artist quality wall arts. Printed on a soft cotton/polyester canvas stretched on a wooden frame. We employ the use of UL Certified Greenguard Gold Ink for every print which provides not only a great quality pigment but also makes the canvas; water, fade, scratch and crack resistant.

    Premium Quality Floating Frames

    Our Roma Mouldings are made from 100% real pine wood, professionally handcrafted in Italy. Every frame is sanded five times to ensure a perfect finish without chipping.

    Best Stretcher Bars in the Industry

    Finger-jointed pine provides enhanced structural stability, durability, and strength.

    Made from sustainable wood from renewable forests (FSC certified)
    Specific size trees are grown to maximize the yield of stretcher bars, which reduces waste during production and result in a workflow and product that is as green as possible.

    We use 1.25" thick warp-free, knot-free, kiln-dried wood from sustainable forests. The frames are finger-jointed and reinforced to make sure they last for many years.
